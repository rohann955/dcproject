The Magnanimity of the Consitution Project

How does our school trip deepen our understanding of the Constitution and our government? By seeing it live in action.

Washington D.C. Trip

White House

On Thursday, we got to see the Bill of Rights First Amendment in action during our visit to the White House. The First Amendment protects 5 basic rights, Freedom of Religion, Freedom of Assembly, Freedom of the Press to report, Freedom of Petition, and Freedom of Speech. We got to see some of these rights being exercised as we observed several protests in designated areas on the street. These included protests of current issues such as the invasion of Ukraine, the Black Lives Matter movement, and a protest by the group Free Tibet.
